How to Use the Seed Pen

Step 1: Use it until finished

Make sure to use the seed pen until the ink is finished. This will leave behind the embedded seed in the pen for planting.

Step 2: Insert the pen upside down

Place the pen upside down in a small pot or garden soil to allow the seed to make contact with the soil for planting.

Step 3: Water regularly into soil

Water the soil regularly, ensuring it stays moist but not waterlogged. The seed will begin to germinate after a few days.
